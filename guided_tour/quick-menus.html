Netscape Guided Tour Menus: Go and Bookmarks
The Netscape application keeps track of pages you have seen and lets you create easy-access lists of favorite pages.

Go Menu
History items from the Go menu bring previously viewed pages. The Netscape application automatically appends the title of a page you have viewed as the topmost menu item in the history list.

Bookmarks Menu
Bookmark items from the Bookmarks menu bring pages of your choice. You can add a bookmark menu item for the page you are viewing by choosing Add Bookmark from the Bookmarks menu.
